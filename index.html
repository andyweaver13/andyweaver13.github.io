My Projects

Breeze IOS App (in progress):

My roommate and I came up with the idea for Breeze when talking with our friends about their difficulties finding off-campus housing. We realized that there is no single platform to facilitate college students finding both housing and other college students to live with. Breeze is our idea of a solution to that problem.

Profile Tab

I created the profile tab to serve as the social media aspect of the app. It allows users to link their Instagram, Twitter, and Facebook accounts as well as listing basic information about themselves and their housing preferences (e.g. House or Appartment).

This is our 'Edit Profile' tab which allows the user to edit their information and profile picture.

The settings screen is not yet functional, however this is a rough framework of how we would like it to look.
