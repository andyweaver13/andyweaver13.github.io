My Projects

Breeze IOS App (in progress):

My roommate and I came up with the idea for Breeze when talking with our friends about their difficulties finding off-campus housing. We realized that there is no single platform to facilitate college students finding both housing and other college students to live with. Breeze is our idea of a solution to that problem.

I created the profile tab to serve as the social media aspect of the app. It allows users to link their Instagram, Twitter, and Facebook accounts as well as listing basic information about themselves and their housing preferences (e.g. House or Appartment).
This is our 'Edit Profile' tab which allows the user to edit their information and profile picture.
We designed our settings screen to be reminiscent of the standard IOS settings screen in order to be more appleaing and familiar to the end user.
The home tab is meant to serve as the hub for the users current home. They can see a countdown timer to when rent is due, pay rent, view their lease, see their current roomates, and message their landlord.
Like the settings tab, we decided to go with a style familiar to iphone users. I believe the minimalist design will create a more user friendly experience for the user.
The map tab is the last tab we began building in Breeze. Right now it is only an interactive map with no search or filtering features, but it is my vision to add these in the near future.
